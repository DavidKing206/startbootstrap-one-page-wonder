Art Club

For those who want a community.

In art club, we play nice relaxing music when students can chill out. There is no requrement on art peices produced only that you have fun!

People have fun here!

The point of Art Club is for you to express your-self. We do not demand you do our project but create your own. If you need our help we will be there. We help students all the time, even the best ones! We help start ideas, but do not give you them.

You do not have to stay.

In Art club we do not demand that you come to every meeting. You can show up when you want. You do not have to stay for the whole time. Our only rule is that if you start a project you finnish it.
